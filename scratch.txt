### 🎨 UI / UX Enhancements (The "Wow" Factor)
1. **Subtle Sound Effects (Audio Feedback):** Visuals are great, but audio makes an app feel alive. We could add very subtle, satisfying sound effects:
   * A satisfying "pop" or pickleball paddle "dink" when you save a score.
   * A crowd cheer when the Champion trophy animation plays.
   * A tiny UI "tick" when navigating between tabs.
2. **Player "Baseball Cards" (Stats Profile):** Right now, clicking a player in the standings shows their match history. We could upgrade this into a beautiful, popup **Player Card** that shows their Avatar, a circular Win-Rate chart, their "Biggest Rival" (the person they lose to the most), and their "Best Partner".
